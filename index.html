Project Idea

Overview

One of the biggest problems that people around the globe (including me) tend to be facing daily seems to be procrastination. I want to design and develop a mobile phone app that gradually removes their habit of leaving things until the last minute by making them create strict schedules or timetables that will push them to complete tasks without delay.

Motivation

This project will be of convenience to everyone who wishes to have a clearer and stricter schedule rather than having their productivity hindered as a result of leaving the to-do until the very last minute. With the current increase in digital devices and the internet, the general procrastination rate is also increasing as people tend to be glued to social media and youtube which consumes most of their time.

Students are one of the major procrastinators as studies show that approximately 80%-95% of university students frequently procrastinate to some extent. As a result, there may be a huge market for student-oriented scheduling mobile applications.

Description

The mobile app’s main purpose is to become a turning point for procrastinators, especially students with their homework. Although this app will be mainly marketed towards students, this is also a great tool for people of all ages who want to be more productive during their day.

The application in mind will run in the background and track the time you spend on your mobile phone and report back to you at the end of the day. The first function you will notice when opening the app is the timetable creator which you will need to fill out for each week and follow. If the time you spend on certain apps like social media or mobile games exceeds a certain threshold every week then the app will automatically set you a goal to reduce screen time which you can work towards. The app will also monitor whether you complete the task you set out to do or not and provide some kind of reward if you do.

The reward to keep the users motivated could be stuff ranging from coupons/vouchers or progress with a certain game/activity that would be implemented to the app. For example, when you hit a certain goal or complete a number of set tasks, your pets in the app will grow or something like that.

Skills Required

To create the software, I will need to learn how to program using kotlin or javascript as the language since they are the most popular language used for mobile applications. I would also need to become familiar with SQL to manage the databases to allow the app users to store their data. Learning the programming languages and becoming familiar with SQL will take quite a while and would be difficult as I have no prior experience with these languages.

Outcome

If this project is successful then countless people will have the opportunity to readjust their habits of procrastination and begin to become much more productive. Gradually fixing their habits will teach them discipline which will make a positive impact on their time management and help them in the long term to avoid procrastination.
